Steps to make a pizza:

  1. Make the dough
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Put sauce on dough
  4. Put cheese on sauce
  5. Put pepperoni on cheese (8 max)
  6. Put whole thing in the oven (300 degrees)
  7. Eat pizza

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
